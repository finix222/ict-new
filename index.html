About River State ICT
The Rivers State Information and communication Technology (ICT) department was created in 2009

The Rivers State Information and Communication Technology (ICT) Department was created in 2008 to support the proactive transformation of the Government and the State economy through ICT for improved e-governance in public service delivery.

Vision/ Mission

The strategic vision of the Rivers State ICT Department is to be a trusted partner in the transformation and delivery of government services that consistently exceed expectations. Our mission is to maximize the use of Information and Communication Technology in order to enhance and sustain service delivery for the overall economic development of the

Mandate
01

Create and maintain the enterprise network infrastructure to provide network connectivity and email to all government offices in Rivers State, as well as technical support for all Rivers State Government websites.

02

Provide continuous training and re-orientation of all civic and public servant, ICT interns and Rivers State residents in the adoption and use of information and communication technology.

03

3. Promote efficiency in Government operations through the automation of Government processes, activities and functions using information and communication technology.

04

4. Develop and implement strategies to grow the information and communication technology sector in Rivers State and work with other bodies to promote the adoption and use of information and communication technology.

INITIATIVES/PROJECTS
01
ICT Farm:

This was the Department’s Computer Summer School for Secondary School Students. It was a free Government initiative geared at capacity building for teenagers. Its focus was to encourage and engage Students at the secondary level of education on the use of basic ICT tools.

02
Basic Digital Literacy Programme:

The drive of this program is to promote Computer Literacy amongst Residents of the State.

03
Codegaminators Program:

This is a multiple Coding, Application, Software and Database training programme designed for youths with the aim of building young entrepreneurial.

04
Court Management Information Systems (Rivcomis)

This is an application software deployed to the Rivers State High Court for a seamless and efficient digitalized court processes.

05
Health Management Information Systems (RivHmis):

This software is deployed to Rivers State Teaching Hospital (RSTH formerly BMH) for the digitization of all the health management processes in the Hospital.

06
Public Service Management Information Systems (RivPusmis):

This is a Database Management System (DBS) structure that facilitates the biometrics enrollment processes and stores biodata of all newly employed, Permanent Staff and Pensioners in the State Public Service.

07
Tax Management Information Systems (RivTamis):

This software was developed for the Rivers State Board of Internal Revenue to comprehensively automate the process of revenue generation through accurate tax payment.

08
Urban Development Management Information System (RivUrban):

This software was developed and deployed to the Ministry of Physical Planning and Urban Development to facilitate e-registration and authentication of every building plan in the state.

09
Education Management Information System (RivEmis):

A software designed and deployed to the Ministry of Education for automation processes in terms of School management operations, School registrations, Results preparations, and certificate disbursement.
